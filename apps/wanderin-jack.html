Sharing Information between Families & Teachers


Inspired by the text-based games of yesteryear, The Adventures of Wanderin' Jack is a story-driven game designed to help kids with ADD/ADHD navigate real-life situations. This work of interactive fiction combines engaging dialogue, decision-making, and visuals to guide players through scenarios that teach self-regulation and problem-solving. Coming Soon!

So what exactly is Wanderin' Jack?

  • Wanderin' Jack is a text-based "adventure" game which aims to walk kids with ADD/ADHD through common real-life situations
  • Through... visuals... and humor, The Adventures of Wanderin' Jack aims to serve as a sort of "practice run" to help kids navigate through everyday situations
  • Wanderin' Jack also has a third quality. But we can't talk about it here because I don't know what it is.
