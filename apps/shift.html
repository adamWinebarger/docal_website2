Dr. Al's ShIFT

Sharing Information between Families & Teachers


Dr. Al's ShIFT (Sharing Information between Families and Teachers) empowers parents, teachers, and caregivers to collaborate effectively by sharing observations and completing tailored questionnaires about a child’s behavior throughout the day. Designed to foster better communication and understanding, ShIFT helps build a comprehensive view of a child’s progress by capturing insights from both home and school environments.

Key features include:

  • The ability for parents and teachers to fill out reports about child behavior over the course of a given day
  • Behavior reports generated in real-time, allowing parents and teachers to look at past child behavior
  • Child behavior over displayed in nice, easy-to-read InfoGraphics

ShIFT is currently in the early trial phase. But if you are interested, be sure to ask Dr. Al about it at your next appointment!
